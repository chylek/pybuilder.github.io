Who built it?

PyBuilder has been created by Alexander Metzner. Past maintainers include Maximilien Riehl and Michael Gruber. As of 2020 Arcadiy Ivanov is actively maintaining PyBuilder.
Some members of the PyBuilder team are using PyCharm by JetBrains. We would like to thank JetBrains for their Open Source License!
